1. Auto Melee
When doing an attack, if the attack is extremely close the the character, a melee attack is performed, this functionality
was taken from the Metal Slug series

2. Scope View
Depending on the weapon equiped, the player is able to push the camera further, for example if the player is holding a sniper rifle
and the move the mouse to the top of the screen it will drag the camera with it quite significantely.

3. Game and GameObject speed, this dictates their movement and animation speeds, basically game speed as well.

4. Game Graphics and Animations

5. BulletTime

6. Blood splatter, when a unit gets shot the blood will come out the other side and collide with the environment like bullets
damaging it with red.
